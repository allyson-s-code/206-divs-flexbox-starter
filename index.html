Hi. I'm Allyson.

I am a Responsive Designer & Developer who loves bringing clean and efficient designs into the world. When I'm not working you can find me painting, exploring the city, out in nature, or baking the best brownies you will ever eat.

I was born and raised in upstate NY and moved around a bit after college. After living all over the country my partner and I settled in Brooklyn, NY, where we are currently raising our son. You can often find us checking out the neighborhood playground.

I have a background in fine art. My first love was painting. For as long as I can remember I have always loved color and drawing. I love the physicality of the materials and creating emotive experiences from the arrangement of shapes, line and color.

More recently I have found web design and development! I love figuring out the puzzle of coding a web page and also that it utilizes some of the same skills that I use in painting to create all sorts of user experiences. And I am learning new things everyday.
